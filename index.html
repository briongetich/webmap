sotik town

Sotik is a town located in the Bomet County of Kenya. It is situated in the western part of the country, approximately 220 kilometers northwest of Nairobi, the capital city of Kenya. The town has a rich history and is known for its significant contributions to Kenya's economic development.

Historically, Sotik was inhabited by the indigenous Kipsigis community, who are part of the larger Kalenjin ethnic group. The Kipsigis people have a strong agricultural background and have long practiced farming in the fertile lands of Sotik. Agriculture remains a vital aspect of the local economy to this day.
